About Study Oracle


StudyOracle is a revolutionary service that enhances studying by using AI technology. Upload PDF files, ask questions, and get relevant answers from the PDFs. It's perfect for law, medical, and engineering students.


Some Usecases

- Upload legal documents and case studies. Ask questions about legal concepts or cases to quickly find relevant passages and get concise answers.
- Upload medical textbooks and research papers. Ask medical queries to receive accurate responses, saving time in finding essential information for coursework or exams.
- Upload engineering textbooks and manuals. Ask questions about formulas, theories, or problem-solving techniques for step-by-step explanations.


How to Use Study Oracle

- Upload PDFs: Add study materials, such as textbooks or research papers.
- Ask Questions: Type clear and specific queries related to your study materials.
- Retrieve Answers: StudyOracle's AI bot scans the uploaded PDFs and provides accurate answers to your questions.
